About Me

Have a particular life obstacle you're trying to overcome? Sometimes, seeking help from a Licensed Therapist is the best way to put things into perspective. Your well-being and mental health is my number one priority. Together we can help you better understand the thoughts and behaviors that might be holding you back from living a more self-aware and fulfilled life.

"Your vision will become clear only when you can look into your own heart. Who looks outside, dreams; who looks inside, awakes." - Carl Jung

Services I Offer

Individual Therapy

In my therapy sessions, we'll work to solve current problems and improve positive thinking and behavior. I will help you develop a greater awareness of your inner psychological process.

Family Therapy

Want to create a closer relationship with family members? Experiencing conflict with a teenage child? Trying to navigate feelings around parenting challenges?

Couples Therapy

Experiencing tension in your relationship or currently facing a crisis and need some guidance? You've come to the right place.

Teletherapy

I offer distance therapy to clients in California and Israel via a confidential online video conferencing platform. Please contact me to find out more about this option.
